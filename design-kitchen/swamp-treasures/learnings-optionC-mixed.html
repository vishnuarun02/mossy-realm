üß™The Great Cursor & Effects Experiment

Tried adding fancy cursors, sparkle trails, fireflies, and click animations. The site turned into a circus.

"More effects ‚â† better vibes. Dense layouts need simple interactions."

What I tried:

  • Pressed flower cursor (opens on hover)
  • Wax seal stamp cursor with click animation
  • Sparkle trail following the cursor
  • Fireflies floating around the page
  • Windows 95 bevel buttons

What I learned:

  • 90s sites were simple for a reason ‚Äî the complexity was in the layout, not the behavior. Modern sites flip this.
  • Animated cursors (.ani) don't work in most browsers. Stick to .cur or .png.
  • The "HD fireflies on grainy page" problem ‚Äî new elements need to match your existing vibe.
  • You can't max out both layout complexity AND interaction complexity.

Decision:

Reverted everything. The site doesn't need fancy effects right now. Less is more.
